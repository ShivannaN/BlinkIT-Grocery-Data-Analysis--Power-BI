# Blinkit Power BI Dashboard

##  Overview

This project presents an **interactive Power BI dashboard** developed to analyze Blinkit’s business performance and operational efficiency.
It provides actionable insights into sales trends, customer behavior, and delivery metrics to support data-driven decision-making and improve overall business performance.

The dashboard allows stakeholders to explore key performance indicators (KPIs) and identify improvement areas across different product categories, regions, and time periods.

---

##  Objectives

* To analyze **overall sales and profit performance** of Blinkit.
* To identify **top-performing product categories and regions**.
* To evaluate **delivery efficiency** and **customer satisfaction metrics**.
* To create an **interactive, dynamic dashboard** using Power BI for real-time insights.

---

##  Dataset Information

The dataset used for this project includes the following fields:

* **Order ID, Date, Outlet Type, Item Type, Item Visibility, Item MRP, Item Fat Content, Outlet Establishment Year, Outlet Size, Outlet Location Type, and Item Outlet Sales.**
* Data was cleaned, transformed, and modeled in Power BI using **Power Query** and **DAX** formulas.

---

##  Key Metrics & KPIs

* **Total Sales Revenue**
* **Average Sales per Outlet**
* **Number of Orders**
* **Profit Margin (%)**
* **Top-Selling Categories**
* **Region-wise Performance**
* **Delivery Efficiency & Timeliness**

---

##  Insights & Findings

* **Supermarket Type 1 outlets** contributed the highest sales volume among all outlet types.
* **Tier 3 cities** performed surprisingly well in terms of order volume, highlighting untapped potential.
* **Fruits and Snacks** emerged as the most profitable item categories.
* Sales showed a **steady upward trend** over recent years with occasional seasonal spikes.
* **Item Visibility** had a direct positive impact on sales performance.

---

##  Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI Desktop** – Data visualization and dashboard creation
* **Power Query Editor** – Data cleaning and transformation
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Calculated columns, KPIs, and measures
* **Microsoft Excel / CSV** – Source data files

---

##  Dashboard Features

* **Interactive Filters & Slicers**: Category, Location, Outlet Type, and Year
* **Drill-through Analysis** for region and outlet-level insights
* **Dynamic Visuals** such as bar charts, line graphs, donut charts, and KPI cards
* **Clean and user-friendly layout** for decision-makers
